Paneer Butter Masala

A creamy and rich dish made with paneer in a buttery tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 500g paneer, cubed
  • 200g butter
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 100ml cream
  • Spices: garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric

Matar Paneer

A delightful combination of green peas and paneer in a spiced tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, garam masala

Dal Makhani

A rich and creamy lentil dish, slow-cooked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/2 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 200g butter
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 100ml cream
  • Spices: cumin, garam masala, coriander
